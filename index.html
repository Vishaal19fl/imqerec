APPLY FOR IMQE SOCIETY

Join the IMQE Society at Rajalakshmi Engineering College and become part of a dynamic community dedicated to fostering excellence in manufacturing and quality engineering. As a member of the Institution of Manufacturing and Quality Engineers, you will have the opportunity to collaborate with like-minded peers and develop skills that are crucial for the future of engineering. Our society offers a platform to transform your creative ideas into tangible solutions, participate in enriching programs, and stay ahead with the latest technological advancements. Apply now and be a part of the journey towards excellence and innovation in manufacturing and quality engineering.

IMQE INTRODUCTION AND VISION

At REC's IMQE Society, we strive to advance the engineering community through exceptional programs, innovative solutions, creative problem-solving, and future-ready technologies.

MISSION STATEMENT

Actively encourage collaboration among Industries, Institutions and future engineers by arranging various technical events that leads to Engineering and societal development.

Promoting and recognizing talent through innovative quality technical meetings and conferences that reinforce new engineering thoughts with quality standards benefiting members of the Institution.
